Since GameUserSettings.ini contains the graphical and gameplay settings along with some personal preferences, I've only included what is used for gameplay. Please copy the section below and replace the [ServerSettings] part of your GameUserSettings.ini. If you would like to keep your mod list for offline games, please copy the line "ActiveMods=(numbers)" from your GameUserSettings.ini into another place and then paste it at the bottom of the [ServerSettings] section of the .ini file. I have included my own mod list below if you would like to use those mods instead. The mods I use are "MarniiMods Hairstyles", "Solo Farm Mod", "Where Are The Bodies - Corpse & Bag Finder", "Vanilla with Sprinkles", "Better Breeding", and "Dino Colourizer!". If you want to use the mods I use, please make sure all of them are installed, then copy the [ServerSettings] section below into your GameUserSettings.ini and Copy the "ActiveMods=(numbers)" line I have included and paste it at the bottom of that section.
